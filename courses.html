Explore Courses for Your Career Path

Courses After 10th

Diploma in Engineering

A 3-year technical course focusing on practical knowledge in various engineering fields like mechanical, civil, electrical, and computer engineering.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: Passed 10th with Science & Math
  • Career Options: Technician, Junior Engineer, Supervisor

ITI (Industrial Training Institute)

Short-term vocational training courses in trades like electrician, plumber, welder, and fitter to prepare for skilled jobs.

  • Duration: 6 months to 2 years
  • Eligibility: Passed 10th
  • Career Options: Skilled Technician, Supervisor, Entrepreneur

Certificate Courses in Computer Applications

Basic computer courses covering MS Office, internet usage, and programming fundamentals.

  • Duration: 3 to 6 months
  • Eligibility: Passed 10th
  • Career Options: Data Entry Operator, Office Assistant

Courses After 12th

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

A 4-year undergraduate degree in engineering fields such as Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, and Electronics.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Eligibility: Passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Math
  • Career Options: Engineer, Developer, Researcher

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)

Undergraduate degree in science streams like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: Passed 12th in Science or Commerce
  • Career Options: Researcher, Lab Technician, Teacher

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Degree focusing on commerce, accounting, finance, and business management.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: Passed 12th in Commerce or Science
  • Career Options: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Entrepreneur

Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

Degree in humanities and social sciences including subjects like Psychology, Sociology, History, and Literature.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Eligibility: Passed 12th in any stream
  • Career Options: Writer, Counselor, Social Worker

Diploma Courses

Diploma in Digital Marketing

Learn SEO, SEM, social media marketing, email marketing, and analytics to build a career in digital marketing.

  • Duration: 6 months to 1 year
  • Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass
  • Career Options: Digital Marketer, SEO Specialist, Content Strategist

Diploma in Graphic Designing

Training in design software like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create visual content.

  • Duration: 6 months to 1 year
  • Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass
  • Career Options: Graphic Designer, UI Designer, Animator

Diploma in Hotel Management

Learn hospitality, food service, and hotel operations for a career in the hotel and tourism industry.

  • Duration: 1 to 3 years
  • Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass
  • Career Options: Hotel Manager, Chef, Event Planner

Other Courses

Certified Financial Planner (CFP)

Professional certification for financial planning, investment, and wealth management.

  • Duration: 6 months to 1 year
  • Eligibility: Graduation or equivalent
  • Career Options: Financial Planner, Investment Advisor

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)

Training in cybersecurity and ethical hacking to protect organizations from cyber threats.

  • Duration: 3 to 6 months
  • Eligibility: Basic IT knowledge
  • Career Options: Cybersecurity Analyst, Ethical Hacker

Foreign Language Courses

Learn languages like French, German, Spanish, or Japanese to enhance career opportunities globally.

  • Duration: 3 months to 1 year
  • Eligibility: Open to all
  • Career Options: Translator, Interpreter, International Business
